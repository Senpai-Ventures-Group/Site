About Us

Energy costs affect the bottom line of all businesses. Till now, it has not been possible for businesses to have a firm grasp on energy costs despite rising prices. This increase in costs is caused by a myriad of factors not limited to a depressed global economic outlook, the rising costs of fuel, the weakening of local currencies vis-a-vis the dollar. It is forseen that these costs may likely increase through to 2050 as countries ramp up grid decarbonization efforts in line with Net-Zero Emmission targets per the Paris Agreement.

We are an energy management company with deep expertise in energy and sensor networks across Africa and India. Our core business is to enable businesses and facilities to have visibility on, and control their energy spend. We do this through a combination of patented sensor hardware and intelligence driven (AI) software tools allowing us to remotely track and manage the most common energy forms used by manufacturers and building owners including grid tied electricity, solar PV and fossil fuels such as petroleum and LPG.

Enabling energy management at scale

By utilizing our industry expertise, networks, low cost sensors and locally driven IP, we are able to increase out value per unit asset, enabling greater impact per dollar. We enable at each business location:

  • 1 Visibiility on energy commodities
  • 2 Energy forecasting
  • 3 Remote or automated control enabling greater energy effeciency
